# 0.1.24 to 0.1.25

- Add a per-website **Backups** tab: on-demand, on-server snapshots of the application root (gzip tar) and any selected databases (`clpctl db:export`), stored under `/home/<user>/backups/<id>/` with a manifest. Backups are created atomically — a failed step removes the partial snapshot — and the newest ten are retained automatically.
- Support **restore** from a snapshot (extract files over the live tree, import databases into their existing CloudPanel databases) with a clear destructive-action confirmation, and **delete** of individual snapshots. Backups take the per-site Operations lock so a heavy job cannot overlap another on the same site.
- Make snapshots reachable for download through the existing **Files** tab (browse to `backups`) or over SSH/SFTP from the **Terminal** tab, avoiding a fragile large-file download path. Backups are only loaded and offered to users who can manage the website.
- Validate `backups` section requests with a strict shared schema (allow-listed action, timestamp-shaped id, database-name and scope constraints) before they reach the bridge, with regression coverage.
- Document the honest scope in-app and in `README.md`/`ARCHITECTURE.md`: snapshots are synchronous and on-server only; Panelavo does not yet manage off-site or scheduled backups, and restore overwrites in place rather than reproducing an exact point-in-time state.
