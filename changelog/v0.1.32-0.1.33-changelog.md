# 0.1.32 to 0.1.33

- Add linked services: a website can expose additional loopback ports of its own stack (API, auth, …) as panel-managed reverse-proxy sites grouped under the parent's Settings tab.
- Linked services stay real CloudPanel reverse-proxy sites with their own domain, aliases, and SSL, but are created with a panel-derived id, site user, and discarded generated password.
- Reject linked-service target ports that are reserved by (or serving) a different website.
- Block deleting a parent website while linked services still exist, and show service sites with a badge nested under their parent in the website list.
- Trim service sites to proxy-relevant sections (settings, domains, vhost, security); operational sections 404 and their nav tabs are hidden.
- Free a deleted website's reserved id/port by removing its panel metadata on deletion.
